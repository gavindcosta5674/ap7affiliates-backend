Commission Rules

Manage commission percentages and view calculations

Formula

Revenue = Deposit - Withdrawal - Bonus

Commission = Revenue × (Percentage / 100)

Example: ₹10,000 - ₹2,000 - ₹500 = ₹7,500 Revenue
₹7,500 × 20% = ₹1,500 Commission
